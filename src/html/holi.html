About this Package:

Join us on an unforgettable journey to immerse yourself in the riot of colors, joy, and spirituality during the legendary Holi festival in the heart of India - Vrindavan, Mathura, and Barsana. Get ready to witness the most vibrant and enchanting celebration of Holi as you explore these sacred towns with rich cultural heritage and immerse yourself in the exuberant festivities.

ITINERARY

ARRIVAL IN VRINDAVAN DAY 1
  • Immerse yourself in the vibrant atmosphere of Holi celebrations.
  • Explore Banke Bihari Temple.
  • Enjoy a traditional dinner at a the restaurant.
  • Proceed to Mathura
MATHURA DAY 2
  • Witness the Lathmar Holi festivities in Mathura, where women playfully beat men with sticks.
  • Join the exuberant crowd in throwing colors and dancing to traditional music.
  • Check in the hotel and rest.
BARSANA DAY 3
  • WProceed for Barsana, the village where Lord Krishna grew up.
  • Explore the historic culture there with the help of local guides.
  • Relish a traditional meal AND check out.
