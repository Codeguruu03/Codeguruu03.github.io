about this Package:

Join us in the tour of what is called the “seven sisters of india” consisting of the narrow valleys beaming with wildlife and the vast water falls and lakes . The tour consists of breathtaking destinations including Assam, Meghalaya, Manipur, Mizoram, Nagaland, Tripura, and Arunachal Pradesh, boast diverse landscapes. Meghalaya's Cherrapunji, known for its rainfall and living root bridges; Assam's Kaziranga National Park, home to one-horned rhinos; and Arunachal Pradesh's Tawang, featuring a stunning monastery, exemplify their unique natural beauty and cultural richness.

So, come on! select from the various tour options and get ready to experience the finest moments in north east India.
