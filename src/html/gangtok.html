Tsomgo Lake (Changu Lake)
This glacial lake located at an altitude of about 12,000 feet is surrounded by snow-capped mountains and is a popular tourist destination. It freezes during the winter months and offers breathtaking views.
Rumtek Monastery
Also known as the Dharma Chakra Centre, this monastery is one of the most significant and largest monasteries in Sikkim. It's a hub of Buddhist activity and artistry.
Namgyal Institute of Tibetology
This institute is dedicated to the study of Tibetan language, culture, and Buddhism. It houses a vast collection of rare artifacts, manuscripts, and thangkas (Tibetan Buddhist paintings).
Ganesh Tok
A small temple dedicated to Lord Ganesha, Ganesh Tok offers panoramic views of the city and surrounding mountains. It's a serene spot for meditation and relaxation.
Enchey Monastery
This monastery, nestled on a hilltop, is around 200 years old and is known for its religious significance and intricate architecture.
Banjhakri Falls and Energy Park
A beautiful waterfall surrounded by lush greenery, this park showcases the traditional culture and folklore of the Lepcha community.
Tashi Viewpoint
From here, you can get a stunning view of the Kanchenjunga mountain range on clear days. It's particularly beautiful during sunrise.
M.G. Marg
The main shopping street of Gangtok, M.G. Marg is a pedestrian-only zone lined with shops, restaurants, and cafes. It's a great place to enjoy local cuisine and shop for souvenirs.
Hanuman Tok
Another temple dedicated to Lord Hanuman, this site offers panoramic views of Gangtok and the surrounding hills
Flower Exhibition Centre
If you're interested in flowers, you'll enjoy this center that showcases a variety of colorful blooms and orchids. The annual flower show held here is a major attraction.
