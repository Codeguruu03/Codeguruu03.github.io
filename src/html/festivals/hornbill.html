About this Package:

Discover the vibrant tapestry of Nagaland's indigenous cultures and traditions on our captivating Hornbill Festival Tour. This 2-day journey will transport you to a world of ethnic diversity, lively celebrations, and authentic experiences as you partake in one of India's most celebrated festivals.

ITINERARY

ARRIVAL IN KOHIMA DAY 1
  • Check in & rest.
  • Attend the colorful and vibrant Hornbill Festival. Experience Naga culture, traditional dances & folk music
  • Enjoy the authentic Naga cuisine.
KHONOMA DAY 2
  • Depart for Khonoma Village.
  • Explore Khonoma's picturesque landscapes, terraced fields, and learn about Naga history and customs.
  • Participate in the closing ceremony of the Hornbill festival
  • Return to Kohima, shop for souvenirs, and overnight stay
