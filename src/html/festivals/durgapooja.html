About this Package:

Embark on an enchanting 2-day journey to West Bengal, where you will be immersed in the vibrant and awe-inspiring celebrations of Durga Puja, one of the most significant and eagerly awaited festivals in India. This meticulously crafted tour offers you the opportunity to witness the devotion, artistry, and cultural richness that define this auspicious occasion

ITINERARY

ARRIVAL IN KOLKATA DAY 1
  • Freshen up and have a traditional Bengali breakfast.
  • Begin your Durga Puja pandal hopping spree, visiting famous pandals like Baghbazar, College Square, and Mohammad Ali Park.
  • Experience the energetic Dandiya Raas dance with colorful sticks.
DURGA PUJA DAY 2
  • Attend cultural events and performances at the pandals
  • Take part in traditional rituals and immerse yourself in the Puja spirit.
  • Enjoy a farewell dinner at a rooftop restaurant with a view of the city skyline
