About this Package :

Experience the enchanting festival of lights, Diwali, in two of India's most sacred cities - Ayodhya and Varanasi. This 2-day tour will immerse you in the vibrant traditions, spiritual significance, and mesmerizing celebrations of Diwali, offering a truly unforgettable cultural experience.

ITINERARY

ARRIVAL IN VARANASI DAY 1
  • Explore the narrow lanes of Varanasi, shop for traditional Diwali items and souvenirs
  • Enjoy a boat ride on the Ganges to witness the illuminated ghats and temples as part of the Diwali celebrations.
  • Witness the Ganga Aarti at Dashashwamedh Ghat, a mesmerizing ritual on the banks of river Ganga.
  • Participate in Diwali festivities, light diyas (earthen lamps), and join in the fireworks display.
ARRIVAL IN AYODHYA DAY 2
  • Drive to Ayodhya, the birthplace of Lord Ram.
  • Visit the Ram Janmabhoomi Temple site and the Hanuman Gadi and soak in the spiritual ambiance.
  • Witness the grand Diwali celebrations in Ayodhya, including elaborate decorations, lighting, and cultural performances.
  • Attend a Ramlila performance to experience the local traditions and cultural heritage.
