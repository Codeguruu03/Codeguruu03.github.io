about this Package:

Join us in the tour of what is called the “heaven on earth”, Jammu and Kashmir, the valleys of Ladakh, and the paradise of Uttarakhand along with a visit to one of the wonders of the world in Agra.

The tour consists of breathtaking destinations including the taj mahal, dal lake, Gulmarg, Leh , Agra fort, Mussoorie and the holy city of Haridwar. Arrangements for a comfortable stay & travel for 13 days & 14 nights are made for you

ITINERARY

Arrival in Jammu & Kashmir DAY 1
  • Arrive in Jammu
  • Visit Raghunath Temple
  • Explore Bahu Fort and Gardens
  • Overnight stay in Jammu
Srinagar and Dal Lake DAY 2
  • Drive from Jammu to Srinagar
  • Visit Dal Lake and enjoy a Shikara ride
  • Explore Nishat Bagh and Shalimar Bagh
  • Overnight stay in Srinagar
Gulmarg and Pahalgam Day 3
  • Day trip to Gulmarg
  • Enjoy Gondola cable car ride
  • Return to Srinagar
  • Drive to Pahalgam
  • Visit Betaab Valley and Chandanwari
  • Overnight stay in Pahalgam
Sonamarg and Zoji La Pass DAY 4
  • Drive from Pahalgam to Sonamarg
  • Enjoy the scenic beauty of Sonamarg
  • Visit Thajiwas Glacier
  • Drive back to Srinagar
  • Overnight stay in Srinagar
Kargil and Dras DAY 5
  • Drive from Srinagar to Kargil
  • Visit Kargil War Memorial in Dras
  • Explore local markets in Kargil
  • Overnight stay in Kargil
Leh DAY 6
  • Drive from Kargil to Leh
  • Visit Leh Palace
  • Explore the local Leh market
  • Overnight stay in Leh
Nubra Valley DAY 7
  • Drive from Leh to Nubra Valley
  • Visit Diskit Monastery and Hunder Sand Dunes
  • Enjoy a camel ride in the desert
  • Overnight stay in Nubra Valley
Pangong Lake DAY 8
  • Drive from Nubra Valley to Pangong Tso
  • Explore the stunning Pangong Lake
  • Overnight camping near Pangong Lake
Leh City Tour DAY 9
  • Drive back to Leh
  • Visit Shey Palace and Thiksey Monastery
  • Explore Hemis Monastery
  • Overnight stay in Leh
Nainital DAY 10
  • Fly from Leh to Nainital
  • Explore the scenic beauty of Nainital
  • Enjoy the traditional cuisine
  • Check in & relax
Rishikesh, Uttarakhand DAY 11
  • Drive from Agra to Rishikesh
  • Visit Laxman Jhula and Ram Jhula
  • Explore Triveni Ghat and attend the Ganga Aarti
  • Overnight stay in Rishikesh
Haridwar and Mussoorie DAY 12
  • Drive to Haridwar
  • Take part in the Ganga Aarti at Har Ki Pauri
  • Visit Mansa Devi Temple
  • Drive to Mussoorie
  • Explore Mall Road and enjoy the scenic beauty
  • Overnight stay in Mussoorie
Dehradun and Departure DAY 13
  • Visit Kempty Falls in Mussoorie
  • Drive to Dehradun
  • Explore Robber's Cave and Sahastradhara
